Thanks for participating in this study!

The research area of this study is Computational Creativity. This research field investigates the phenomenon of creativity in computing machines. The presented project is a novel approach in computational linguistics, which aims to research on interactive storytelling systems.

This particular part of the research aims to identify the contribution of user interaction in a creative performance. There are no risks involved in taking part in this research study and you can change your mind at any stage and withdraw from the study.
